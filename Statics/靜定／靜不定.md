## Equilibrium in 2D

- $\sum{F_{x}}=0$
- $\sum{F_{y}}=0$
- $\sum{M_{z}}=0$

## Trusses

1. m + r - 2n = ?
	1) > 0, Statically Indeterminate
	2) = 0, Statically Determinate
	3) < 0, Partially Constraints
		m = members
		n = unknowns
		r = reactions 
1. Frame → Simple Trusses = Free Bodies
	1) each Free bodies, $\sum{F_{x}, F_{y}, M_{z}}=0$
2. Improperly Constraints 
	1) all reactions are ==Parallel== or
	2)  ==Concurrent==
