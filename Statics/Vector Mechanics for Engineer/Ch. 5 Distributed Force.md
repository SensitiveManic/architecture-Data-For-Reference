## 1. Center of Gravity of Area & Lines

![[Center of Gravity of Area & Lines.png]]

- $\sum_{i=1}^{N}\Delta{W_i}$
- $\sum{M_y}=\overline{x}·w=\sum{\Delta{x_i·W_i}}$
  $\sum{M_x}=\overline{y}·w=\sum{\Delta{y_i·W_i}}$
- $\overline{x}=\frac{\sum{\Delta{x_i·W_i}}}{W}=\frac{\int{xdw}}{\int{dw}}$
  $\overline{y}=\frac{\sum{\Delta{y_i·W_i}}}{W}=\frac{\int{ydw}}{\int{dw}}$
- Center of Gravity = $(\overline{x},\overline{y})$

## 2. Centroids & Center of Gravity

![[Distributed Loads on Beams.png|300]]

- $\Delta{W}=\rho\\gt\Delta{A}$
  ${W}=\gamma\\tA$
- $\rho$ = Density
  $t$ = Thickness
  $\gamma=\rho\\g$
- $\sum{M_y}=\overline{x}·w=\int{xdw}=\int{x\gamma\\tdw}A$
  $\overline{x}·\gamma\\tA=\gamma\\t\int{xdA}$
-  $\overline{x}=\frac{\int{xdA}}{A}$
   $\overline{y}=\frac{\int{ydA}}{A}$
- Centroids = $(\overline{x},\overline{y})$
- if homogeneity, Centroids = Center of Gravity

## 3. First Moment of Area

 - $Q_x=\int{ydA}$, 對Ｘ軸的moment
 - $Q_y=\int{xdA}$, 對Ｙ軸的moment

## 4. Determination of Centroids by Integration

- $dA=dxdy$
- $\overline{x}_{el}=x$ → dA的重心
  $\overline{y}_{el}=y$
- $Q_x=\int{y_{el}dA}$
  $Q_y=\int{x_{el}dA}$
- $\overline{x}=\frac{\int{x_{el}dA}}{\int{dA}}$
  $\overline{y}=\frac{\int{y_{el}dA}}{\int{dA}}$
