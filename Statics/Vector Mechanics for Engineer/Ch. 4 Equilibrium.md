## Statically Indeterminate
#### 1. Statically Indeterminate
- unknowns > number of equations
#### Statically determinate
- unknowns = number of equations
#### 2. Partially Constraints
- unstable
- unknowns < number of equations
#### 3. Improperly Constraints
- 1. all reactions are ==**Parallel**== or
- 2. ==**Concurrent**==
