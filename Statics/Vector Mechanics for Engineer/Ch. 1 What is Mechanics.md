## What is Mechanics

- Rigid Bodies : **==Statics==**, Dynamics
- Deformable Bodies : Mechanics of Material
- Fluid : Fluid of Mechanics

## Concept

- Space
- Time
- Mass(or Force)

## Six Fundamental Principles

- Parallelogram Law
- Transmissibility
- Newton's 1st, 2nd, 3rd laws
- Newton's law of Gravitation

## System of Units

- SI Units(International System of Units)
	- N : kg · m/s2
	- Length : m
	- Mass : kg
	- Time : sec
	
- U.S Customary Units
	- 1 kip = 1000 lbf(1000磅力) ≈  4.448222 kN
	- Length : ft
	- Force : lbf
